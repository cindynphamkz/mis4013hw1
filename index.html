What is the MBTI?

The MBTI categorizes individuals into 16 personality types based on four dichotomies: Extraversion vs. Introversion, Sensing vs. Intuition, Thinking vs. Feeling, and Judging vs. Perceiving.

History of MBTI

Developed by Isabel Briggs Myers and her mother, Katharine Cook Briggs, the MBTI is rooted in Carl Jungâ€™s theory of psychological types.

The Four Dichotomies

  • Extraversion (E) vs. Introversion (I)
  • Sensing (S) vs. Intuition (N)
  • Thinking (T) vs. Feeling (F)
  • Judging (J) vs. Perceiving (P)

Applications of MBTI

MBTI is widely used in personal development, career counseling, team building, and relationship counseling.
