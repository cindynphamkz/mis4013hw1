MBTI Personality Types

There are 16 unique personality types in the MBTI framework. Each type has its own characteristics and preferences.

Type Overview

Explore the characteristics of different types such as INTJ, ESFP, and more. Each type offers a unique perspective on personality.

Type Descriptions

  • INTJ:The Architect – Strategic, independent, and analytical.
  • INTP: INTP: The Thinker – Innovative, logical, and curious.
  • INFJ: INFJ: The Advocate – Insightful, compassionate, and idealistic.
  • INFP: The Mediator – Idealistic, empathetic, and introspective.
  • ISTJ: The Logistician – Responsible, detail-oriented, and reliable.
  • ISTP: The Virtuoso – Practical, adventurous, and resourceful.
  • ISFJ: The Defender – Protective, supportive, and practical.
  • ISFP: The Adventurer – Artistic, flexible, and sensitive.
  • ENTJ: The Commander – Assertive, strategic, and efficient.
  • ENTP: The Debater – Quick-witted, curious, and original.
  • ENFJ: The Protagonist – Charismatic, inspiring, and altruistic.
  • ENFP: The Campaigner – Enthusiastic, imaginative, and sociable.
  • ESTJ: The Executive – Organized, practical, and strong-willed.
  • ESFJ: The Entrepreneur – Bold, perceptive, and direct.
  • ESFP: The Consul – Social, supportive, and nurturing.
  • ESFP: The Entertainer – Energetic, outgoing, and spontaneous.
