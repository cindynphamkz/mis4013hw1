Take the MBTI Test

Find out which of the 16 MBTI personality types best describes you by taking our online test. Answer these fun hypothetical questions honestly for the most accurate results and see which answer best matches yours!

Instructions

Read the four scenarios and after thinking about your answer, click the answers below to see which letter matched yours the most! There are no right or wrong answers, just responses that best fit your personal preferences.

Question 1

Do you prefer to work in a team setting or work alone?

Question 2

What are your first thoughts when you imagine a red apple?

Question 3

This question is scenario based: You get a text from a close friend saying "hey, i just got the craziest hair cut on a whim because im so sad." What is your first thoughts/message back to them?

Question 4

Before traveling to a new destination for vacation, do you tend to make an itinerary?

Answers for Question 1

Extrovert(E): "Teams of course! The more the merrier!" | Introverts(I): "I like to complete tasks on my own and at my own pace."

Answers for Question 2

Sensing(S): "Yum! That's a red fruit for sure." | Intuitive(N): "Is the apple symbolizing special meanings like teachers, evilness, temptation..."

Answers for Question 3

Thinking(T): "Send a picture of the haircut... also are you alright?" | Feeling(F): "oh no, are you okay?? what happened?"

Answers for Question 4

Perceiving(P): "NOOO, i like to go with the flow and figure out places to go when im there"| Judging(J): "DUH! How else will I get to see all the touristy spots during my short time there!
